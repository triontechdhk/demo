Who We Are

Bridging Youth & Policy

The IGCFoundation was born out of a simple yet powerful idea: young people shouldn't just inherit the future; they should shape it.

Based in Dhaka, we are Bangladesh‚Äôs premier youth-led think tank dedicated to analyzing public policy, international relations, and governance through a fresh lens. In a world where decision-making often feels disconnected from the next generation, we act as the bridge.

Our team consists of researchers, activists, and young professionals working tirelessly to decode complex political landscapes. We believe that by arming the youth with data, dialogue, and diplomacy, we can build a more transparent and sustainable Bangladesh.

Our Mission

To empower the next generation of leaders with the tools to influence national governance and global cooperation.
